about me.


Hello there, my name is Luis Cermeno. I'd like to thank you for visiting my personal page. Here you will learn about me and what I do. I'm currently majoring in Computer Science, and I love web development. I have a thing for building cohesive designs and a comfy user experience.

For some, progamming is just technology-aid problem solving but for me is more than that. I would describe it as an art of creating. Like painters paint landscapes that are pleasant to people's eyes, like musicians write songs that evoke emotions, I strive to build my programs with the most attractive and efficient interface that can enhance the user experience.

On vacations, I love roadtripping and hiking. Some life-time moments can come from the right hikes.
