about me.


Hello there! Well, formally I am a computer science student but in my daily basis I am a learning lover. My passion for learning new things has brought me all the way from my beloved Peru and given me the courage to pursue my passion.

I remember all began with a HP Prime calculator and the tedious problem of finding the geometric centroid of combined shapes. I just could'nt stand the fact that I was doing the same thing over and over so I decided to hop into the programming world for the first time and write a simple script that could do the work for me. And when I finally finished it... man! the satisfaction that grows inside you is like watching your kid growing up.

I would describe my passion as something more than "coding", it is my need for creation that drives me crazy. As I'm writing this webpage (that for my fortune you are reading) I'm into web-design, but a few months ago I was crazier for solving engineering problems using Matlab. And if God allows me to, soon I will be learning more and more stuff to build a big project I have in mind.
